Lasagna

Description

This is lasagna. It's your favourite, you know it is. Perfect if your name is garfield

Ingredients

Red Sauce

  • 1-2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 400g mince beef
  • 1-2 small cans of tomatoes
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1-2 bay leaves
  • salt & pepper

Mornay Sauce

  • 2-4 tbsps flour
  • 50g butter
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 250g cream
  • 250g milk
  • 250g yellow cheese
  • nutmeg
  • pepper

Steps

  1. preheat the oven to 180, warm up the milk in a pan
  2. Fry the aromatics for the red sauce and add the tomato paste and oregano
  3. Fry the mince beef on one one side. Flip it over once brown
  4. Break it up and add the tomatoes, sugar and bay leaves and leave to simmer
  5. In another pan, melt the butter and oil. Once hot, add in the flour
  6. Whisk until the smell of fresh bread fills the kitchen
  7. Stir in the warm milk and cream, whisking the whole time
  8. Once incorporated, work in the nutmeg and pepper
  9. Gradually add cheese to the mornay, stirring the whole time and tasting
  10. In the lasagna pan, add the follow layers:
    • red (small amount)
    • sheets
    • red (1/3 of total)
    • sheets
    • red (1/3)
    • white (1/2)
    • sheets
    • red (1/3)
    • sheets
    • white (1/2)
    • cheese
  11. Bake for 45 min covered in foil, then for 5-10 on maximum heat for the crispiness
  12. Let cool for 15 min and serve
