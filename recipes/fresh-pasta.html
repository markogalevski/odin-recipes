Fresh Egg Pasta

Description

A fresh egg pasta which can be made quickly and rolled out either by hand or machine

Ingredients

per serving

  • 100g flour
  • 1 egg
  • pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Pile your flour on your clean work surface and mix in your salt
  2. Create a deep well with your flour. You should see a lot of your work surface surrounded by a high walled ring of flour
  3. Crack in your eggs
  4. Start beating the eggs with a fork. Once it's one colour, start collapsing the well bit by bit into the eggs, beating all the while.
  5. Once the mixture becomes too thick to beat, it's time to knead
  6. Work the dough until it becomes a supple and tack like dough
  7. Wrap in cling foil/place in a small airtight container and let rest for 25min
  8. Roll out either with your pasta machine or a rolling pin.
