Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Serves 4

This is how Marco Guercini taught me to make carbonara. It's the very legit and real roman way, and not the americanised cream version.

Ingredients

  • 4 egg yolks
  • black pepper
  • 200g pecorino romano
  • 150g fresh pasta per person (see my fresh pasta recipe)
  • 100-150g guancale or bacon

Steps

  1. Set water to boil for the pasta. Salt the water VERY well.
  2. Chop up and fry the guancale/bacon in a hot pan. Drain the rendered fat and pieces into a separate bowl. Set aside.
  3. Beat the egg yolk with the finely grated pecorino and black pepper
  4. Once the water is boiling, add and cook the fresh pasta for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Once it's ready, remove the pasta with tongs and add to the pan you cooked the guancale in.
  6. Add in half a ladle of pasta water and, after letting it cool very slightly, throw in your egg mixture
  7. Whisk and toss and turn like crazy to get an emulsion. The residual heat of the pasta should cook the egg without scrambling it
  8. Add in the guancale fat and stir once more.
  9. Plate, top with the guancale, some more pepper, and some more pecorino.
  10. Enjoy!
